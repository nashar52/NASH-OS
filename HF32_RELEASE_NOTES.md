# NASH OS HF32 — DISTINCT OPERATIONAL APPLICATIONS

This corrective release replaces the repeated Select Employee / closed-loop / AI template for primary employee and HR navigation pages.

## Dedicated employee applications
- Attendance & Timesheet: clock, month calendar, exceptions, overtime and attendance actions.
- Tasks & Evidence: task queue, deadlines, evidence vault and task actions.
- Employee File: employee profile, employment details, file health and document library.
- Performance: review cycle, objectives, competencies, development and trend.
- Rights & Reports: entitlements, employee services and request history.

## Dedicated HR applications
- Performance Center
- Learning Center
- Compensation Center
- Government Compliance Center
- Quality & Governance Center
- Explainable AI Decision Support

Each domain has its own title, KPIs, queue columns, hierarchy and primary action. No database schema, migration, reset or seed is included.
