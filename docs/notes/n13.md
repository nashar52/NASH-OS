## What Build 14 Adds
Build 14 adds a Quality & Governance Operating Center that turns operational activity into governable controls:

- Quality checks
- Governance gates
- Control failure register
- Corrective actions / CAPA
- Evidence requests
- Audit receipts
- AI governance explanation only


- Approve Gate
- Return Gate
- Reject Gate
- Export Quality Report

## AI Rule
AI can recommend, explain, and warn. AI cannot approve a governance gate, close a control failure, bypass evidence, or alter schema.
